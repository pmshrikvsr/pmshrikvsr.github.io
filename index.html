Our School

PM SHRI
Kendriya Vidhyalaya
Sevoke Road

Kendriya Vidyalaya Sevoke Road is a school in the city of Siliguri, West Bengal, India, offering both primary and secondary education. The school is situated in Salugara locality, in the foothills of Himalayas. It is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, and run by Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan. It was built in defence land, and opened in the year 1981. Later the school was shifted to a new building few hundred metres away.

Environment

Kendriya Vidyalaya Sevoke Road, located in Siliguri, West Bengal, is a tranquil learning environment.

Excellence

The school provides primary and secondary education, maintaining high educational standards.

Dedication

It caters to children of transferable Central Government employees, striving for educational excellence.

Creativity

Equipped with modern facilities, it promotes innovation and organizes various co-curricular activities.
